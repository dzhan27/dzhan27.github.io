Experience

Undergrad Research Assistant @ Fuchs Group

Sept 2021 - Present | Ithaca, NY

I'm currently developing a computational model to describe the quantum properties of nitrogen-vacancy centers in diamond's lattice structure (whose properties are well understood). This model will then hopefully shed light on the properties of boron vacancy centers, which we know little about.

Physics Lab Technician Intern @ Honeywell

Jun 2021 - Aug 2021 | Broomfield, Colorado

I developed a Surface Ion Trap Chip tester to automate testing of Honeywell Quantum’s surface electrode traps. This program is written in Python and interfaces with NI measurement chasse to measure said traps' electrical characteristics. It includes a live hardware calibration, capacitance and resistance tests, a histogram plotter to visualize data, and a GUI for less software-savvy users. It reduces testing time by over 90%, personnel needed to test the trap by 100% (wow!), and scales linearly with trap size - which is important when Honeywell Quantum makes larger and more sophisticated ion traps.

Software Engineer @ Cornell Mars Rover

Sept 2020 - Present | Ithaca, New York

I worked to improve the Rover's Hardware Abstraction Layer's messaging efficiency to reduce control lag, and am currently planning out an implementation of Inverse Kinematics for the Rover’s arm to enable intelligent endpoint movement without manually writing to individual motors.
