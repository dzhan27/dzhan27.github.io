About Me

I'm Daniel Zhan, an undergrad studying Engineering Physics and Computer Science at Cornell University. Some of my technical interests include space, quantum computing, robotics, and video game development. I work on software to enable intelligent arm movement and efficient communications with the Cornell Mars Rover project team during the school year, and am developing a program to automate circuit verification for qubit ion trap PCBs at Honeywell Quantum through summer 2021. I also love playing badminton and doing cardio at the gym in my free time.
